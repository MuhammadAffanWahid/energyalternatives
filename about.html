About Energy Alternatives

Energy Alternatives, founded in 2012, is a top solar provider in Pakistan, delivering renewable energy solutions across residential, industrial, agricultural, and commercial sectors. Our team, with decades of experience, has rapidly built a strong reputation.

We are unique in Pakistan for our on-site testing facility using European equipment to ensure design and product quality. With extensive expertise and in-house capabilities in design, fabrication, and installation, we are dedicated to reliability and customer satisfaction, continuing to serve clients since our inception.

A Message from Our CEO

I am honored to address you as the CEO of Energy Alternatives, a pioneer in renewable energy solutions. At Energy Alternatives, we are driven by a singular vision: to power a sustainable future for generations to come.

In today’s rapidly evolving world, the need for clean, reliable energy has never been more pressing. Climate change, environmental degradation, and resource depletion are urgent challenges that demand innovative solutions. As stewards of the planet, it is our responsibility to lead the way towards a greener, more sustainable future.

At Energy Alternatives, we are committed to harnessing the power of alternative energy sources such as solar PV & Thermal, wind, hydro, and biomass. Through cutting-edge technology, rigorous research, and unwavering dedication, we are transforming the energy landscape one project at a time. But our mission extends beyond simply providing renewable energy. We are dedicated to fostering strong, lasting partnerships with our clients, built on trust, transparency, and mutual respect. Together, we can achieve remarkable feats and create a world where energy is abundant, affordable, and clean.

As we embark on this journey together, I invite you to join us in our pursuit of a brighter, more sustainable future. Together, let us power progress, empower communities, and pave the way for a world that thrives on clean, renewable energy. Thank you for your continued trust and support.

- Eng. Muhammad Altaf, CEO
